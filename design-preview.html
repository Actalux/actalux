What this is

Clayton school board meetings and public documents, made searchable — with every quote traceable to its source.

Actalux is a nonpartisan, nonprofit archive of the public records of the Clayton, Missouri school district: board meetings, minutes, budgets, curriculum documents, resolutions, and records obtained through Sunshine Law requests. Every passage on this site is a verbatim quote with its source citation attached. We add nothing to the record. We take nothing away. Read our content policy.

Board of Education — Regular Meeting, November 13, 2024

Budget presentation. Dr. Ferguson presented the operating budget update. The operating budget reflects a net increase of $4.2 million, driven primarily by negotiated salary and benefit increases and by the expansion of the multilingual learner program.

Board discussion followed. Ms. Oduya requested clarification on the projected reserve fund balance at year-end. Dr. Ferguson responded that current estimates place the reserve balance at approximately 28.7 percent, consistent with the district's reserve-target policy.
