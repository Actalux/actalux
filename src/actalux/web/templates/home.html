What this is

Clayton school board meetings and public documents, made searchable — with every quote traceable to its source.

Actalux is a nonpartisan, nonprofit archive of the public records of the Clayton, Missouri school district: board meetings, minutes, budgets, curriculum documents, resolutions, and records obtained through Missouri Sunshine Law requests. Every passage on this site is a verbatim quote with its source citation attached. We add nothing to the record. We take nothing away. Read how this works.
